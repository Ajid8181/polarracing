Our Story

Founded in 2008, Polar Racing emerged from a passion for pushing the boundaries of automotive excellence. What began as a small team of racing enthusiasts in Milan has grown into an internationally recognized brand synonymous with innovation, performance, and luxury.

Our journey started on the racetrack, where we honed our engineering skills and developed technologies that would later define our production vehicles. Today, Polar Racing represents the pinnacle of automotive craftsmanship, combining Italian design with cutting-edge technology.

With each model we create, we strive to deliver an unparalleled driving experience that connects the driver to the machine in the most visceral way possible. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond our vehicles to our global community of passionate owners and enthusiasts.

Our Mission

At Polar Racing, our mission is to create extraordinary automotive experiences that ignite passion and inspire innovation. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of engineering excellence while maintaining an unwavering focus on sustainable performance.

We believe in the power of human ingenuity and the thrill of the open road. Each vehicle we create is a testament to our dedication to perfection, from the drawing board to the racetrack.
